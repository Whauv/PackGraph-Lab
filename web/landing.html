What PackGraph does

Investigate packaging decisions as a connected operating system, not a set of disconnected tools.

PackGraph is built for teams that need to move from evidence search to decision output without losing traceability, context, or operational realism.

Lead capability

Search and understand the full packaging decision graph.

Teams can search across materials, suppliers, regulations, documents, and reports, then immediately follow the relationships that explain risk, evidence coverage, and substitution paths.

How the workflow works

Built as a process, not just a feature list.

Product areas

Three focused workspaces instead of one overloaded dashboard.

Each area supports a different stage of the decision path, so teams can start light and move deeper only when the question deserves it.

Why it is different

Designed for evidence-connected reasoning, not generic recommendation theater.

PackGraph treats materials, suppliers, regulations, documents, and operational signals as linked entities. That makes it easier to explain why a decision is viable, what evidence supports it, and what might break next quarter.
